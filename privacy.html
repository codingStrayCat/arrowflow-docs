This Privacy Policy describes how the mobile application Arrow Flow: Mind the Order (the "App"), developed by Jaehee Kim ("we", "us", or "our"), handles information when you use the App. We respect your privacy and aim to collect as little information as possible.

1. Information We Collect Ourselves

The App itself does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information to our servers. All gameplay state (e.g. level progress, sound/haptic settings) is stored locally on your device using the operating system's standard preference storage and is never sent to us.

We do not operate our own servers to which the App transmits your data.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

The App integrates the following third-party services, which may collect information as described in their own privacy policies. You are encouraged to review them.

2.1 Google AdMob (advertising)

The App displays advertisements served by Google AdMob, a service operated by Google LLC. AdMob may collect and process the following, subject to Google's privacy practices:

  • Your device's Advertising ID (AAID on Android)
  • IP address and coarse location derived from it
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, network type)
  • Ad interaction events (impressions, clicks)
  • Approximate app usage data required for ad frequency capping

This data is used to deliver, measure, and improve advertising. For users in the European Economic Area, United Kingdom, and Switzerland, ads are served using Google's Consent Management Platform, and you will be asked for consent (personalized vs. non-personalized ads) on first launch.

You can reset or opt out of your Advertising ID at any time through your device's system settings:
Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID.

Google's Privacy & Terms: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.2 Google Play Services

The App is distributed through the Google Play Store. Google Play may collect diagnostic information such as crash reports and basic device metadata per its own terms, independently of the App. See Google Play's privacy practices at play.google.com/about/play-terms .

3. Permissions Used

  • INTERNET — required to request and render advertisements.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — required by the AdMob SDK to check network availability before requesting ads.
  • com.google.android.gms.permission.AD_ID — allows the AdMob SDK to read the advertising identifier.

The App does not request access to contacts, location services, the camera, microphone, storage, or any other sensitive permission.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction, such as 14 in South Korea or 16 in certain EEA countries). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

Because the App integrates Google AdMob with standard ad requests, it is not suitable for audiences under the above ages and has not been submitted to Google Play's "Designed for Families" program.

5. Your Rights

5.1 Users in the European Economic Area / United Kingdom (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, as well as the right to data portability. The legal basis for processing advertising data is your consent, obtained via Google's Consent Management Platform. You may withdraw consent at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App.

5.2 Users in California (CCPA / CPRA)

California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, the right to request deletion, the right to correct inaccurate information, and the right to opt out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal information. The App itself does not sell your information. Ad-related data processed by Google AdMob may constitute "sharing" under California law; you may opt out via adssettings.google.com .

5.3 Users in South Korea (개인정보보호법)

Under the Personal Information Protection Act of South Korea, you have the right to access, correct, delete, and suspend the processing of your personal information. Requests may be sent to the contact address below. The personal information manager ("개인정보 보호책임자") is:

  • Email: jaeheekim.dev@gmail.com

6. Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal information on our own servers, we retain none. Information collected by Google AdMob and Google Play is retained by Google according to its retention policies.

7. International Data Transfers

Our third-party service providers (Google) operate globally and may process data in the United States or other jurisdictions. By using the App, you acknowledge such transfers, which are governed by the service providers' own safeguards (including Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable).

8. Security

While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we and our third-party service providers use industry-standard measures to protect the limited data described above. Because the App stores only local preferences on your device, the primary safeguard is your own device's security.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Material changes will be communicated through an in-app notice or an update to the App's store listing.

본 개인정보처리방침은 모바일 애플리케이션 Arrow Flow: Mind the Order(이하 "본 앱")의 개발자 김재희(이하 "당사")가 본 앱 이용과 관련하여 개인정보를 어떻게 처리하는지를 안내합니다.

1. 당사가 직접 수집하는 정보

본 앱은 이용자를 식별할 수 있는 개인정보를 당사의 서버로 수집, 저장, 전송하지 않습니다. 게임 진행 상황 및 사운드/햅틱 설정과 같은 내부 상태는 단말기 운영체제의 기본 저장 기능을 통해 단말기 내부에만 보관되며, 당사에 전달되지 않습니다.

2. 제3자 서비스가 수집하는 정보

본 앱은 아래 제3자 서비스를 통합하고 있으며, 각 서비스의 개인정보 처리는 해당 서비스의 정책을 따릅니다.

2.1 Google AdMob (광고)

본 앱은 Google LLC가 운영하는 AdMob을 통해 광고를 표시합니다. AdMob은 광고 게재, 측정, 품질 개선을 위해 다음 정보를 수집할 수 있습니다: 광고 식별자(AAID), IP 주소, 기기 정보(모델, OS 버전, 언어), 광고 노출 및 클릭 이벤트, 빈도 제한을 위한 앱 사용 데이터.

이용자는 설정 → Google → 광고 → 광고 ID 삭제를 통해 광고 ID를 언제든지 재설정하거나 맞춤 광고를 차단할 수 있습니다. 자세한 사항은 Google 개인정보처리방침을 참고하시기 바랍니다.

2.2 Google Play 서비스

본 앱은 Google Play를 통해 배포되며, Google Play는 크래시 리포트 및 기본 기기 메타데이터 등을 Google 자체 약관에 따라 수집할 수 있습니다.

3. 사용 권한

  • INTERNET — 광고 요청 및 표시를 위해 필요합니다.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE — AdMob SDK가 네트워크 상태를 확인하기 위해 필요합니다.
  • AD_ID — AdMob SDK가 광고 식별자를 읽기 위해 필요합니다.

본 앱은 연락처, 위치, 카메라, 마이크, 저장소 등 민감한 권한을 요청하지 않습니다.

4. 만 14세 미만 아동의 개인정보

본 앱은 만 14세 미만 아동을 대상으로 하지 않으며, 아동의 개인정보를 의도적으로 수집하지 않습니다. 본 앱은 표준 광고 요청을 사용하므로 Google Play의 "가족용(Designed for Families)" 프로그램 대상이 아닙니다.
